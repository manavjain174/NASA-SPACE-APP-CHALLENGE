Orbital debris is a 'Junk' that is circling Earth.There nuts,bolts, spent rocket stages and broken pieces of satellites orbiting Earth are just a few of the many thousands of items known as orbital debris, or space junk.

Most "space junk" is moving very fast. It can reach speeds of 4.3 to 5 miles per second. Five miles per second is 18,000 miles per hour. That speed is almost seven times faster than a bullet.
